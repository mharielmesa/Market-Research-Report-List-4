<p><p>A diaphragm buffer tank is a type of pressure vessel used in water supply systems to maintain stable pressure levels and prevent water hammer. These tanks are equipped with a flexible diaphragm that separates the water chamber from the air chamber, allowing for the expansion of water without causing pressure fluctuations. Diaphragm buffer tanks are commonly used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications to improve the efficiency and longevity of water distribution systems.</p><p>The diaphragm buffer tank market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7% during the forecast period.
