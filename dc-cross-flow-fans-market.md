<p><p>DC cross flow fans come in two main types - single shaft and double shaft. Single shaft fans have a single motor shaft connected to the impeller, providing a more compact design while still maintaining efficient airflow. Double shaft fans have two motor shafts, offering greater stability and airflow control. Both types are commonly used in various applications such as electronics cooling, HVAC systems, and industrial equipment where compact size and high efficiency are important factors.</p></p>
