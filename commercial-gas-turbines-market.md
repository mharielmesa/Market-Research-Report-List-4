<p><p>Commercial gas turbines are categorized into two main types based on their axle configuration: single axis and dual axis market. Single axis turbines have a single shaft that connects the compressor and turbine, allowing for a simple and compact design. Dual axis turbines have two separate shafts for the compressor and turbine, providing greater flexibility in power output and efficiency. The choice between single axis and dual axis turbines depends on the specific requirements and operating conditions of the application.</p></p>
