<p><ul><li>High Junction Capacitive Type</li><li>Low Junction Capacitive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ESD TVS diodes are used in electronic devices to protect against electrostatic discharge. High junction capacitive type diodes have a higher capacitance, making them more effective at absorbing high levels of energy during a discharge event. On the other hand, low junction capacitive type diodes have a lower capacitance, making them more suitable for applications where space is limited and lower clamping voltages are required. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
