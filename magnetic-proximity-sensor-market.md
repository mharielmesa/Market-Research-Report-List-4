<p><p>Magnetic proximity sensors are available in two main types: fixed distance sensors and adjustable distance sensors. Fixed distance sensors have a set sensing range that cannot be changed, making them ideal for applications where a consistent detection distance is required. On the other hand, adjustable distance sensors allow the user to manually change the sensing range, offering more flexibility in various applications. Both types of sensors are widely used in industrial automation, automotive, and consumer electronics industries for detecting the presence or absence of objects.</p></p>
