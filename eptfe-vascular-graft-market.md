<p><p>ePTFE vascular grafts are categorized into two main types based on their caliber size: small caliber and large caliber. Small caliber ePTFE vascular grafts are primarily used for procedures involving smaller blood vessels, such as those in the extremities, while large caliber grafts are more commonly used for larger vessels, such as those in the heart or aorta. Each type of graft is designed to provide durability, flexibility, and biocompatibility for optimal performance in different vascular applications.</p></p>
