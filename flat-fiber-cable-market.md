<p><p>Flat fiber cables are utilized in various applications in the telecommunications industry. The market for flat fiber cables can be categorized into Single-Mode and Multi-Mode types. Single-Mode cables have a smaller core size and are designed for long-distance transmission of data, making them suitable for high-speed applications. On the other hand, Multi-Mode cables have a larger core size and are used for shorter distances but offer a cost-effective solution for data transmission within buildings or data centers. Both types cater to different needs in the market.</p></p>
