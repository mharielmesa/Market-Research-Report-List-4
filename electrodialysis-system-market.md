<p><p>Electrodialysis system market consists of two main types: Continuous Electrodialysis and Batch Electrodialysis. </p><p>Continuous Electrodialysis involves a continuous flow of feed solutions through the system, allowing for a continuous separation process. This type is preferred for large-scale operations and industries requiring constant production.</p><p>On the other hand, Batch Electrodialysis involves the separation of feed solutions in batches, making it suitable for smaller-scale operations or when precise control over the separation process is needed. Both types offer efficient and effective separation of ions from solutions.</p></p>
