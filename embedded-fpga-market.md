<p><p>EEPROM, Antifuse, SRAM, Flash, and others are the different types of Embedded FPGA markets based on the configuration technology used. EEPROM involves rewriting the configuration multiple times, Antifuse is a one-time programmable technology, SRAM allows for reprogramming as it retains data as long as power is applied. Flash technology offers a balance between flexibility and non-volatility. Other types may include programming technologies like fuse-based or mixed technologies. Each type offers specific advantages and trade-offs based on the application requirements.</p></p>
