<p><ul><li>Firmware over-the-air (FOTA)</li><li>Software over-the-air (SOTA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Over-The-Air (OTA) Update Market consists of two main types: Firmware OTA (FOTA) and Software OTA (SOTA). FOTA involves updating the embedded software on electronic control units within a vehicle, while SOTA deals with updating applications and operating systems in the vehicle's infotainment system. Both FOTA and SOTA allow manufacturers to remotely update and improve a vehicle's features, functionality, and performance without requiring the vehicle to be physically brought to a dealership for maintenance.</p></p>
