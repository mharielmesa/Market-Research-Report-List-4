<p><p>The seal adhesive tape market is categorized into single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided adhesive tapes have adhesive on one side, making them ideal for mounting, insulation, and packaging applications where only one surface requires adhesion. In contrast, double-sided adhesive tapes feature adhesive on both sides, allowing for bonding two surfaces together seamlessly, commonly used in displays, automotive, and construction. Both types cater to diverse industries, providing solutions for various applications and enhancing efficiency in assembly and sealing processes.</p></p>
