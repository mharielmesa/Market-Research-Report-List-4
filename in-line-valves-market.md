<p><p>In-line valves are devices used to control the flow of fluid in a pipeline. They come in three main types: Manual valves are operated by hand, providing simple on/off control. Pneumatic valves use compressed air to regulate flow, offering automation and remote operation. Electromagnetic valves use electrical signals to open and close, ideal for precise control and integration with electronic systems. Each type of valve has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs in various industries.</p></p>
