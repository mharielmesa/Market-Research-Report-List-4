<p><strong>The Dental High Speed Handpiece Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inside Exhaust Handpiece</li><li>Outside Exhaust Handpiece</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental high-speed handpiece market is divided into two primary types: inside exhaust handpieces and outside exhaust handpieces. Inside exhaust handpieces expel air and debris internally, minimizing the spread of contaminants, making them preferred in sterile environments. In contrast, outside exhaust handpieces vent air and debris externally, which can be advantageous for visibility and cooling during procedures. Each type caters to specific dental procedural requirements, influencing the choice based on factors like hygiene, ease of use, and patient safety.</p></p>
