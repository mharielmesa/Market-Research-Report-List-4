<p><p>Wireless fire detection systems are divided into two main types: Addressable and Conventional. </p><p>Addressable wireless fire detection systems allow for individual devices to be identified and communicate with the control panel, providing specific information about the location of a fire or fault. </p><p>Conventional wireless fire detection systems, on the other hand, use zones to indicate the general location of a fire or fault, making them a simpler and more cost-effective option for smaller buildings or areas. Both types offer reliable and efficient fire detection and alarm solutions.</p></p>
