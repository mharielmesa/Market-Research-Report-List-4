<p><p>Check valves are designed to allow flow in one direction and prevent backflow in the opposite direction. There are two main types of check valves commonly found in the market: swing check valves and lift check valves. Swing check valves have a hinged disc that swings open and closed to control flow, while lift check valves have a disc that lifts up to allow flow and drops down to prevent backflow. Other types of market include ball check valves, stop-check valves, and dual plate check valves.</p></p>
