<p><p>Lipid peroxidation assay kits are used to measure the levels of lipid peroxidation in biological samples. There are two main types of these kits available in the market: Colorimetric and Fluorometric. Colorimetric kits measure lipid peroxidation by detecting a color change, while fluorometric kits measure it by detecting fluorescence intensity. Both types of kits provide a simple and reliable method for researchers to accurately quantify lipid peroxidation levels in various samples, making them essential tools in studying oxidative stress and related diseases.</p></p>
