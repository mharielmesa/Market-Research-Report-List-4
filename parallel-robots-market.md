<p><p>Parallel robots are classified into two main market types based on the number of axes they operate on: multi-axis and two-axis robots. Multi-axis robots have more than two degrees of freedom, allowing for greater flexibility and range of motion in industrial applications. Two-axis robots, on the other hand, are simpler in design with only two degrees of freedom, making them ideal for specific tasks that require only two-dimensional movement. Both types of robots offer unique advantages and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
