<p><strong>The Handheld VOC Gas Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld VOC gas detectors come in different types, including PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor. PID detectors work by ionizing gas molecules to detect VOCs, making them ideal for detecting a wide range of volatile organic compounds. On the other hand, Metal-oxide Semiconductor detectors operate by detecting changes in electrical resistance when exposed to VOCs, offering a cost-effective solution for gas detection. Both types of detectors play a crucial role in ensuring safety in various industries where VOC exposure is a concern.</p></p>
