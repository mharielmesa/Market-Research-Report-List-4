<p><p>Innerspring mattresses come in two main types: double-sided and single-sided. They are preferred by those who want a more traditional design with the ability to rotate for even wear. Single-sided innerspring mattresses have comfort layers only on one side, which cannot be flipped and are typically more affordable. They are popular among those looking for a lower maintenance option with adequate comfort and support.</p></p>
