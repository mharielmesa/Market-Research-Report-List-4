<p><p>The vibrating screen machine market can be categorized into two types: free vibrating and constrained vibrating. In free vibrating screens, the movement of the screen is not restricted, allowing for more efficient screening of materials. On the other hand, constrained vibrating screens have limitations on the movement of the screen, which can lead to more accurate and precise screening results. Both types have their own unique advantages and are used in various industrial applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
