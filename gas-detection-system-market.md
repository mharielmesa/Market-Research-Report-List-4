<p><p>Gas detection systems are crucial for detecting and monitoring toxic and combustible gases in various industries. The market for gas detection systems includes four main types: Semiconductor Type, Electrochemical, Catalytic Combustion, Photoionization (PID), and Infrared Sensor. Semiconductor Type systems are based on the conductivity of gas; Electrochemical systems use chemical reactions to detect gases; Catalytic Combustion systems detect combustible gases through a catalytic reaction; PID systems use UV light to ionize gases; and Infrared Sensor systems detect gases based on their absorption of infrared radiation.</p></p>
