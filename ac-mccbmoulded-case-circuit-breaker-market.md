<p><p>Moulded Case Circuit Breakers (MCCBs) come in two main types, Thermal Magnetic MCCBs and Electronic Trip MCCBs. Thermal Magnetic MCCBs use both thermal and magnetic mechanisms to detect and respond to overcurrents, providing reliable protection for electrical systems. On the other hand, Electronic Trip MCCBs use electronic components for faster and more precise overcurrent detection and response. Both types offer efficient and effective circuit protection, but Electronic Trip MCCBs are often preferred for more advanced or sensitive electronic systems.</p></p>
