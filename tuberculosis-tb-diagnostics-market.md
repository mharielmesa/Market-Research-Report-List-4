<p><p>The Tuberculosis (TB) diagnostics market comprises several key types. Culture-based diagnostics involve growing TB bacteria from patient samples to confirm infection, offering high accuracy but requiring longer turnaround times. Sputum smear microscopy uses stained sputum samples to visually identify TB bacteria, providing quick results, though with lower sensitivity. Rapid molecular diagnostics utilize advanced technologies to detect TB DNA, delivering faster and more precise results. Other diagnostic methods may include skin tests and blood tests, contributing to comprehensive TB management.</p></p>
