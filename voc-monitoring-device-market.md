There are two main types of VOC monitoring devices in the market: Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor sensors. PIDs are effective for detecting a wide range of VOCs by ionizing molecules in the gas sample, while Metal-oxide Semiconductor sensors use a chemical reaction to detect and measure VOC levels. Both types of devices are crucial for ensuring workplace safety and environmental compliance.</p></p>
