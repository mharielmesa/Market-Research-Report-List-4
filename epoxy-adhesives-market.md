<p><p>Epoxy adhesives market can be categorized into three main types: One-component, Two-component, and Others. One-component epoxy adhesives are ready to use and do not require mixing before application, making them convenient for quick bonding applications. Two-component epoxy adhesives consist of separate resin and hardener components that need to be mixed before use, providing stronger and more durable bonds. The Others category includes specialty epoxy adhesives with unique formulations tailored for specific industrial applications.</p></p>
