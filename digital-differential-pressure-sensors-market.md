<p><strong>The Digital Differential Pressure Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Silicon Based</li><li>Foil Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital Differential Pressure Sensors are classified into two main types based on their technology: Silicon Based and Foil Based. Silicon Based sensors use micro-electromechanical systems (MEMS) technology to measure pressure differentials, offering high accuracy and reliability. Foil Based sensors, on the other hand, use a thin metal diaphragm that deforms with pressure changes, providing a cost-effective solution for lower accuracy requirements. Both types offer unique advantages and are widely used in various industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
