<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Polystyrene Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Polystyrene Capacitors</li><li>Radial Polystyrene Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polystyrene capacitors are categorized into two main types: axial and radial. Axial polystyrene capacitors feature leads extending from either end, allowing for through-hole mounting in circuit boards, ideal for applications requiring stability and low distortion. Radial polystyrene capacitors have leads positioned on the same side, facilitating compact designs and PCB space-saving. Both types exhibit excellent temperature stability and low dielectric losses, making them suitable for high-frequency and precision applications in electronics, including audio equipment and timing circuits.</p></p>
