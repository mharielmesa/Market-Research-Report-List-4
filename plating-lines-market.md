<p><p>Plating lines are classified into rack plating lines, barrel plating lines, and other types in the market. Rack plating lines are used to hang individual parts on racks for plating, ensuring uniform coating on all surfaces. Barrel plating lines, on the other hand, are designed for small, loose parts that are tumbled in a barrel during the plating process. Other types of plating lines may include continuous plating lines, reel-to-reel plating lines, and selective plating lines, each serving specific industrial requirements and processes.</p></p>
