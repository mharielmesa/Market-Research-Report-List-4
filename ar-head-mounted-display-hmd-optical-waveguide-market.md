<p><p>The AR head mounted display (HMD) optical waveguide market includes three main types: Geometric Waveguide, Diffractive Waveguide (Surface Relief Grating), and Diffractive Waveguide (Volume Holographic Gratings). Geometric waveguides use total internal reflection to guide light, while diffractive waveguides utilize surface relief or volume holographic gratings to diffract and guide light. Each type has its own advantages and applications in AR technology, leading to a diverse market where manufacturers can choose the best waveguide type based on their specific needs.</p></p>
