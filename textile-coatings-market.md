Thermosets are materials that undergo a chemical reaction when heated, leading to irreversible crosslinking and hardening. The "Others" category may include coatings made from materials such as silicones or natural polymers. Each type of coating offers unique properties and benefits, allowing manufacturers to tailor their products to specific performance requirements in various industries.</p></p>
