<p><p>Linear voltage regulators are widely used in various applications for stabilizing the output voltage of power supplies. There are two main types of linear voltage regulators: Standard and Low Dropout (LDO) regulators. Standard regulators provide a fixed output voltage regardless of changes in input voltage or load, while LDO regulators offer a more precise and regulated output voltage with lower dropout voltage. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining voltage stability and ensuring reliable operation of electronic devices in a wide range of industries.</p></p>
