<p><ul><li>Single Bridge Box Trucks</li><li>Double Bridge Box Trucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Box trucks are categorized into two primary market types: single bridge and double bridge box trucks. Single bridge box trucks feature a single axle configuration, making them ideal for urban deliveries and navigating tight spaces, offering better fuel efficiency and maneuverability. In contrast, double bridge box trucks have a dual axle design, allowing for higher load capacities and stability on highways, making them suitable for long-haul transportation and larger deliveries. Each type serves distinct operational needs based on cargo requirements.</p></p>
