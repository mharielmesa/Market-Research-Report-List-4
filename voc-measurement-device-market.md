<p><strong>The VOC Measurement Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VOC Measurement Devices are classified into different types based on the technology used. PID (Photoionization Detection) devices measure volatile organic compounds by ionizing gases using ultraviolet light. Metal-oxide Semiconductor devices detect VOCs through the change in conductivity of a sensor when exposed to gas. PID devices are known for their high sensitivity and fast response time, while Metal-oxide Semiconductor devices are more durable and have a longer lifespan. Both types are commonly used in industrial and environmental monitoring applications.</p></p>
