<p><p>Microplate incubators are essential equipment in biological and chemical laboratories for culturing cells or bacteria in microplates. Single-layer incubators are compact and ideal for small-scale experiments, while multi-layer incubators offer higher capacity and allow for simultaneous incubation of multiple microplates. Single-layer incubators are suitable for individual researchers or small labs, while multi-layer incubators are preferred by larger research facilities or institutions with high throughput requirements. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining optimal conditions for cell growth and experimental accuracy.</p></p>
