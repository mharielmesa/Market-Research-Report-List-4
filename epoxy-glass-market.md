<p><strong>The Epoxy Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lay-up</li><li>Compression Molding</li><li>Resin Injection</li><li>Resin Transfer Molding</li><li>Filament Winding</li><li>Pultrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Epoxy glass market types include lay-up, compression molding, resin injection, resin transfer molding, filament winding, and pultrusion. Lay-up involves manually placing layers of resin-saturated fabric onto a mold. Compression molding uses heat and pressure to cure the epoxy in a mold. Resin injection involves injecting resin into a closed mold. Resin transfer molding uses a two-part mold and a vacuum to distribute resin evenly. Filament winding involves winding resin-saturated fibers onto a rotating mandrel. Pultrusion pulls resin-saturated fibers through a die to produce continuous profiles.</p></p>
