<p><p>Cold Plasma Solution Market includes two types: Atmospheric Cold Plasma and Low Pressure Cold Plasma. Atmospheric Cold Plasma utilizes room air as the working gas, making it suitable for surface treatment applications. On the other hand, Low Pressure Cold Plasma operates at a reduced pressure using a specific gas, making it ideal for more precise and controlled applications such as semiconductor manufacturing. Both types of cold plasma offer unique benefits and are used in various industries for different purposes.</p></p>
