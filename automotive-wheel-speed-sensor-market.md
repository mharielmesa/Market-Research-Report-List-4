<p><p>Automotive Wheel Speed Sensor market is categorized into two types - Passive Sensor and Active Sensor. Passive sensors rely on external forces (like the rotation of the wheel) to generate a signal, while active sensors use their own power source to generate a signal. Passive sensors are typically simpler and more cost-effective, while active sensors tend to offer higher accuracy and performance. Both types play a vital role in gathering crucial data on wheel speed for applications like anti-lock braking systems and traction control systems in vehicles.</p></p>
