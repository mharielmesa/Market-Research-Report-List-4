<p><p>Etch process market types refer to the two main methods used for removing material from a surface: dry etching and wet etching. Dry etching involves using plasma or reactive gases to selectively remove material, making it ideal for high-precision and delicate applications. On the other hand, wet etching uses liquid chemicals to dissolve the material, making it more suitable for bulk material removal. Each market type caters to different industries and applications based on their unique characteristics and requirements.</p></p>
