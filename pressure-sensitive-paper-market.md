<p><p>Pressure sensitive paper markets can be classified into various types based on their adhesion levels. HHS (high-high shear) market includes papers with very high adhesion, suitable for rough surfaces and heavy-duty applications. HS (high shear) market consists of papers with strong adhesion for general purpose applications. MS (medium shear) market offers moderate adhesion for versatile use. LW (low shear), LLW (lower low shear), LLLW (lowest low shear), and 4LW markets provide decreasing levels of adhesion ideal for temporary applications or easy removal.</p></p>
