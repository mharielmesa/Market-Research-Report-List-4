<p><strong>The UV-Curable Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Base-coat</li><li>UV Mid-coat</li><li>UV Top-coat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV-Curable Coatings Market includes three types of coatings: UV Base-coat, UV Mid-coat, and UV Top-coat. UV Base-coat is applied as the first layer on a substrate to enhance adhesion and provide a durable foundation for subsequent coatings. UV Mid-coat is applied between the base-coat and top-coat to enhance color, gloss, and durability. UV Top-coat is the final layer applied to provide protection, aesthetic appeal, and resistance to wear and tear. Each type of coating plays a crucial role in ensuring the overall quality and performance of the finished product.</p></p>
