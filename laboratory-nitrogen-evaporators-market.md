<p><p>Laboratory nitrogen evaporators are used to concentrate samples by evaporating solvent under a gentle nitrogen gas flow. There are two main types of laboratory nitrogen evaporators - metal dry bath and water bath evaporators. Metal dry bath evaporators use a metal block to provide heat to the samples, while water bath evaporators use heated water to maintain a consistent temperature. Both types of evaporators are commonly used in laboratories for various applications such as sample preparation, solvent evaporation, and concentration of samples.</p></p>
