<p><p>The uncooled IR detector chip market consists of two main types, based on the technology used: amorphous silicon and vanadium oxide. Amorphous silicon technology offers lower cost and power consumption, making it suitable for applications such as surveillance cameras and thermography. Both technologies play a crucial role in advancing the capabilities of IR detectors in various industries.</p></p>
