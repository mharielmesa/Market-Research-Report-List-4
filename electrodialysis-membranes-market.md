<p><p>Electrodialysis membranes are categorized into three main types based on their structure and composition. Heterogeneous membranes consist of different layers with varying properties, while homogeneous membranes have a uniform composition throughout. Bipolar membranes have both cation and anion exchange layers in a single membrane. Each type of membrane offers unique advantages and applications in electrodialysis processes for various industries such as water treatment, food and beverage production, and pharmaceuticals. The choice of membrane type depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
