<p><p>The Lubricant Skid market includes two main types: Mobile Lube Skid and Fixed Lubricating Oil Skid. Mobile Lube Skids are portable units that can be easily transported to different locations for on-site lubrication services. They are commonly used in industries with multiple vehicles or equipment that require regular maintenance. Fixed Lubricating Oil Skids, on the other hand, are stationary units installed at a specific location for centralized lubrication servicing.
