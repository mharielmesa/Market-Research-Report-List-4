There are two main types of markets for this process: SMC (Sheet Moulding Compound) and BMC (Bulk Moulding Compound). SMC market consists of products made from a mixture of different resins, fibers, and fillers, while BMC market involves using a higher concentration of fillers. Both markets offer durable and aesthetically pleasing coatings for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
