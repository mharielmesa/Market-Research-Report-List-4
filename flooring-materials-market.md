<p><p>The flooring materials market can be divided into three main types: resilient, non-resilient, and soft cover. Resilient flooring includes materials like vinyl, linoleum, and rubber that are durable and able to bounce back from impact. Non-resilient flooring consists of materials like ceramic tile, porcelain tile, and hardwood that do not have much flexibility or give. Soft cover flooring includes materials like carpet and area rugs that provide a comfortable and cushioned surface underfoot. Each type of flooring material offers different benefits and characteristics for various applications.</p></p>
