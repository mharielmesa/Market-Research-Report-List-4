<p><p>Spinal retractors are surgical instruments used to hold back soft tissues and provide access to the operative area during spinal surgeries. There are two main types of spinal retractors: tubular retractors and blade retractors. Tubular retractors consist of a cylindrical tube with adjustable blades that can be inserted into the surgical site, while blade retractors feature flat, sharp blades that can be positioned to hold tissues out of the way. Both types of retractors are essential tools for ensuring clear visibility and safe access during spinal procedures.</p></p>
