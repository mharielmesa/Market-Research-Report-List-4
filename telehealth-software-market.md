<p><p>Telehealth software offers three main market types: Real-Time Communication, Store-and-Forward, and Remote Patient Monitoring. Real-Time Communication allows patients and healthcare providers to interact instantly through video conferencing or messaging. Store-and-Forward involves sharing patient information, such as images or test results, with a specialist for diagnosis at a later time. Remote Patient Monitoring enables healthcare professionals to track and monitor patients' vital signs and health data from a distance, facilitating continuous care and early intervention.</p></p>
