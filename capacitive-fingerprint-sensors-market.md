<p><strong>The Capacitive Fingerprint Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area</li><li>Swipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitive fingerprint sensors are categorized primarily into area and swipe types. Area sensors capture the entire fingerprint image at once, offering high-resolution scans for authentication, commonly found in smartphones and secure entry systems. In contrast, swipe sensors require users to swipe their fingers across the sensor, capturing fingerprint data gradually. This type is often used in compact devices where space is limited. Both technologies leverage capacitive sensing to accurately detect and authenticate fingerprints, enhancing security in various applications.</p></p>
