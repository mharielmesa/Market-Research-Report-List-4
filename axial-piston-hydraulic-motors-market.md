<p><strong>The Axial Piston Hydraulic Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swash Plate</li><li>Bent Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Axial piston hydraulic motors come in two main types: swash plate and bent axis. Swash plate motors have a fixed-angle swash plate that controls the angle of the pistons. This design offers high efficiency and smooth operation. On the other hand, bent axis motors have pistons that move in a bent path relative to the drive shaft, allowing for a compact design and high power density. Both types are commonly used in various industrial applications for their reliability, durability, and performance.</p></p>
