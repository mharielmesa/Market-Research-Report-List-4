<p><p>Bias Ply and Radial Ply are two types of airplane tires that differ in their construction. Bias Ply tires have cords that run diagonally across the tire, providing flexibility and rugged performance. They are usually cheaper than Radial Ply tires but tend to have a shorter lifespan. Radial Ply tires have cords that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better stability and fuel efficiency. They are more expensive but provide a smoother ride and better overall performance. These two types cater to different needs and preferences in the airplane tire market.</p></p>
