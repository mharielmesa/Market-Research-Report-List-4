<p><p>Collagen dressings are available in various types, including antimicrobial collagen dressings, alginate collagen dressings, hydrogel collagen dressings, native collagen dressings, and others. Antimicrobial collagen dressings are designed to prevent infections, while alginate collagen dressings are used for wound exudate management. Hydrogel collagen dressings provide moisture to the wound bed, promoting healing. Native collagen dressings are made from natural collagen fibers. Other types of collagen dressings may include composite dressings or collagen powder. Each type caters to different wound care needs and preferences.</p></p>
