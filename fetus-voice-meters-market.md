<p><p>Fetus voice meters are used for monitoring the heartbeat of the fetus during pregnancy. There are two main types of fetus voice meters - Doppler fetus-voice meters and passive fetus-voice meters. Doppler fetus-voice meters use ultrasound technology to detect and amplify the fetal heartbeat, offering real-time audio feedback. Passive fetus-voice meters, on the other hand, use acoustic sensors to pick up the sounds of the fetal heartbeat without the need for ultrasound. Both types of meters are popular in the market for monitoring fetal health during pregnancy.</p></p>
