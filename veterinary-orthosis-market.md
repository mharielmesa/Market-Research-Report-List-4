<p><p>Veterinary orthosis market primarily consists of two main types: forelimb and hindlimb orthoses. Forelimb orthoses are designed to support and treat conditions affecting the front limbs of animals, such as arthritis, injuries, or deformities. On the other hand, hindlimb orthoses are specifically tailored for treating conditions in the hind limbs of animals, such as hip dysplasia, cruciate ligament injuries, or nerve damage. Both types of orthoses aim to improve mobility, reduce pain, and enhance the overall quality of life for animals in need.</p></p>
