<p><p>Digital Temperature Indicators come in two main market types: fixed and portable. Fixed indicators are permanently installed in a specific location and provide continuous temperature monitoring. These are commonly used in industrial settings where temperature control is critical. Portable indicators, on the other hand, are designed to be easily moved from one location to another for temporary temperature readings. This type is often used in field applications such as HVAC maintenance, food processing, and healthcare industries. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining quality and safety standards.</p></p>
