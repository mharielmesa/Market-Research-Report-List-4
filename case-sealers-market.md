<p><p>Case sealers are commonly categorized into two main types: Fully Automatic Carton Sealers and Semi-Automatic Carton Sealers. </p><p>Fully Automatic Carton Sealers are advanced machines that can automatically adjust to various box sizes and seal them efficiently without the need for manual intervention. They are ideal for high-volume production lines where speed and consistency are essential.</p><p>On the other hand, Semi-Automatic Carton Sealers require some manual assistance in feeding and positioning the boxes before sealing. They are suitable for smaller production lines or businesses with lower packaging volume.</p></p>
