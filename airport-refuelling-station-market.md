<p><strong>The Airport Refuelling Station Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tank</li><li>Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airport refuelling stations can be categorized into two main types - tank market and line market. Tank market involves refuelling aircraft directly from large storage tanks located on-site at the airport. This method is commonly used for smaller airports with limited fuel demands. On the other hand, line market involves refuelling aircraft through a network of fuel pipelines connected to the airport. This method is more commonly used in larger airports with higher fuel demands and allows for more efficient and continuous refuelling operations.</p></p>
