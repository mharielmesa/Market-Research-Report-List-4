<p><p>Alzheimer's Disease treatment market includes various types of medications such as monoamine oxidase inhibitors, pain inhibitors (acetylcholinesterase inhibitors), and glutamate inhibitors. Monoamine oxidase inhibitors work by increasing levels of neurotransmitters in the brain. Pain inhibitors help improve cognitive function by preventing the breakdown of acetylcholine. Glutamate inhibitors regulate the levels of glutamate, which is associated with nerve cell damage in Alzheimer's patients. These different types of medications aim to manage symptoms and slow down the progression of the disease.</p></p>
