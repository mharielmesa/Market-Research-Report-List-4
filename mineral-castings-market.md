<p><strong>The Mineral Castings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Epoxy Mineral Casting</li><li>Polymer Minreal Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mineral Castings market includes two main types: Epoxy Mineral Casting and Polymer Mineral Casting. Epoxy Mineral Casting utilizes epoxy resins combined with minerals like quartz sand to create durable, high precision components used in various industries such as automotive and aerospace. On the other hand, Polymer Mineral Casting involves the use of polymer resins mixed with minerals to produce lightweight, cost-effective parts with excellent damping properties. Both types of mineral castings offer superior performance and versatility in various applications.</p></p>
