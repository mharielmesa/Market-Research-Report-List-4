<p><p>The inflatable life jackets market includes two main types: vest type and yoke type. Vest type life jackets are designed like a traditional vest with straps around the waist and shoulders, providing a secure fit. Yoke type life jackets, on the other hand, feature a yoke that goes around the neck and shoulders, distributing the buoyancy evenly across the chest and back. Both types are popular choices for water sports and boating activities, offering safety and peace of mind for users.</p></p>
