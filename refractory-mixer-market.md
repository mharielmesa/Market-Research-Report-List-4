<p><p>Refractory mixers are categorized into two main types: horizontal mixers and vertical mixers. Horizontal mixers are typically used for large-scale mixing applications where the materials need to be continuously mixed and discharged. Vertical mixers, on the other hand, are more suitable for smaller batches and offer better control over the mixing process. The choice between horizontal and vertical mixers often depends on the size of the project and the specific requirements of the mixing process.</p></p>
