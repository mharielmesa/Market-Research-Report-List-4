<p><ul><li>Pressure Controlled Ventilator</li><li>Volume Controlled Ventilator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure Controlled Ventilator in the Small Animal Ventilators Market provides ventilation by delivering a set pressure to the airways during inspiration. This type of ventilator is beneficial in patients with poor compliance or resistance in their lungs. Volume Controlled Ventilator, on the other hand, delivers a set volume of air to the patient's lungs during each breath. This type of ventilator is commonly used in small animals to ensure consistent and controlled ventilation. Both types play a crucial role in managing respiratory function in small animals.</p></p>
