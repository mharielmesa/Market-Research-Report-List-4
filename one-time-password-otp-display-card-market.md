<p><ul><li>OTP Magnetic Card</li><li>OTP Chip Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The One Time Password (OTP) Display Card market comprises primarily two types: OTP Magnetic Cards and OTP Chip Cards. OTP Magnetic Cards are equipped with a magnetic strip that generates a time-sensitive code for secure transactions. In contrast, OTP Chip Cards utilize embedded microchips to produce one-time passwords, providing enhanced security features and resistance to cloning. Both types aim to enhance online transaction security by ensuring that each password is unique and valid for a limited time, reducing the risk of unauthorized access.</p></p>
