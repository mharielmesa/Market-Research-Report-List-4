<p><p>Power Factor Correction Capacitors (PFCCs) are essential devices used in electrical systems to improve the power factor, which is a measure of how effectively electrical power is being converted into useful work output. Low power factor can lead to efficient energy consumption and increased energy costs, making PFCCs critical in optimizing energy use in industrial and commercial applications. They work by reducing reactive power demand, leading to enhanced system efficiency, reduced losses, and lower electricity bills.</p><p>The Power Factor Correction Capacitors Market is anticipated to grow significantly, with an expected CAGR of 10.90% during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include rising energy costs, the increasing adoption of renewable energy sources, and stringent government regulations aimed at improving energy efficiency.
