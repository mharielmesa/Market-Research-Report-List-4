<p><p>Corrugated paperboard can be classified into different types based on the number of layers it contains. Single wall paperboard consists of one layer of fluted paperboard sandwiched between two flat layers. Double wall paperboard has two layers of fluted paperboard and three flat layers. Triple wall paperboard contains three layers of fluted paperboard and four flat layers. Other types of corrugated paperboard include various combinations of fluted and flat layers to meet specific packaging requirements. Each type offers varying levels of strength and durability for packaging applications.</p></p>
