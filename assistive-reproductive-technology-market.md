<p><p>Assistive Reproductive Technology market includes various types such as In-vitro fertilization (IVF), Artificial Insemination-Intrauterine Insemination (AI-IUI), Frozen Embryo Replacement (FER) and others. IVF involves fertilizing an egg with sperm outside of the body and then implanting the resulting embryo into the uterus. AI-IUI involves placing sperm directly into a woman's uterus to facilitate fertilization. FER involves using frozen embryos for implantation. Other types may include gamete intrafallopian transfer (GIFT) and zygote intrafallopian transfer (ZIFT). Each method has its own unique benefits and considerations for individuals seeking fertility assistance.</p></p>
