<p><p>Fluorocarbon rubber sheets are categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I fluorocarbon rubber sheets are known for their excellent resistance to swelling in harsh chemical environments, making them ideal for applications where exposure to chemicals is a concern. On the other hand, Type II fluorocarbon rubber sheets are designed to offer high tensile strength and abrasion resistance, making them suitable for applications that require durability and toughness under extreme conditions. Both types cater to different needs within the market for specialized rubber materials.</p></p>
