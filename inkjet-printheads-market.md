<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Print Heads</li><li>Thermal Print Heads</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet printheads market is segmented into piezoelectric print heads, thermal print heads, and others. Piezoelectric print heads use pressure generated by piezoelectric materials to eject ink droplets. Thermal print heads use heat to vaporize the ink and create droplets. The "others" category may include technologies such as continuous inkjet printing. Each type of printhead has its own advantages and disadvantages, making them suitable for different applications and industries within the inkjet printing market.</p></p>
