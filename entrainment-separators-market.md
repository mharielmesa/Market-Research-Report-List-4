<p><p>Entrainment separators are used in various industries to remove liquid droplets from gas or vapor streams. There are two main types of entrainment separators: wire-mesh and baffle-type. Wire-mesh separators use a series of screens to capture droplets, while baffle-type separators have plates or baffles that cause droplets to impinge and coalesce. Both types are effective in separating entrained liquid from gas streams, with each having its own advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
