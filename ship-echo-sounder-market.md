<p><strong>The Ship Echo Sounder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dual Frequency Echo Sounder</li><li>Single Frequency Echo Sounder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ship echo sounder market is segmented into two major types: dual frequency echo sounder and single frequency echo sounder. Dual frequency echo sounders use two different frequencies simultaneously to provide more detailed and accurate depth measurements in varying water conditions, making them ideal for professional marine applications. On the other hand, single frequency echo sounders are more commonly used for general depth measurements in shallower waters and are typically more affordable for recreational boating purposes.</p></p>
