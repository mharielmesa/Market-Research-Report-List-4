<p><p>Glass balustrade systems are available in two main types including structural glass balustrades and frameless glass balustrades. Structural glass balustrades are installed with the support of a handrail or supporting structure, providing added strength and stability. On the other hand, frameless glass balustrades are fixed directly to the floor or stairs without any visible support, creating a sleek and modern look. Both options are popular choices for contemporary architecture and interior design projects, offering safety, durability, and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
