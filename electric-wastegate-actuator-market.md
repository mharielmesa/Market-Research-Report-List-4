<p><p>Electric Wastegate Actuator Market can be categorized into two main types: Linear Electric Wastegate Actuator and Rotary Electric Wastegate Actuator. </p><p>Linear Electric Wastegate Actuator operates in a straight line motion to control the wastegate valve in turbocharged engines, providing precise and efficient control over exhaust gas flow. </p><p>On the other hand, Rotary Electric Wastegate Actuator uses a rotary motion to regulate the wastegate valve, offering a different method of controlling exhaust gas flow in turbochargers. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
