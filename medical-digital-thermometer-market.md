<p><p>The medical digital thermometer market consists of two main types: electronic and infrared thermometers. Electronic thermometers utilize sensors to measure body temperature and display the results on a digital screen. They are easy to use and provide quick readings. On the other hand, infrared thermometers measure temperature through the heat emitted by the body without direct contact. They are non-invasive, hygienic, and suitable for all age groups. Both types of thermometers are essential tools for monitoring and managing health conditions.</p></p>
