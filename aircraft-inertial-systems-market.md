<p><p>AHRS type refers to Attitude and Heading Reference Systems that provide attitude and heading information for aircraft navigation. INS type, or Inertial Navigation Systems, use gyroscopes and accelerometers to determine aircraft position and orientation. IMU type stands for Inertial Measurement Units, which provide accurate motion sensing capabilities. Laser type systems use laser technology for precise navigation and positioning. Other market types include advanced sensor technologies and integrated systems that improve aircraft navigation and control. These systems play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient flight operations.</p></p>
