<p><p>Exhaust gaskets market consists of various types including exhaust manifold gaskets, exhaust flange gaskets, and header gaskets. Exhaust manifold gaskets are used to seal the connection between the exhaust manifold and the cylinder head. Exhaust flange gaskets seal the connection between exhaust components, such as the exhaust pipe and the muffler. Header gaskets are designed to seal the connection between the exhaust headers and the engine block. Each type of gasket serves a specific purpose in ensuring a tight seal and preventing exhaust leaks.</p></p>
