<p><p>EV Battery Thermal Management System is crucial for maintaining optimal temperature range to ensure battery performance and lifespan. There are two types of systems: Active Management System and Passive Management System. Active systems use cooling/heating mechanisms like liquid cooling, air cooling, etc., to regulate temperature actively. Passive systems, on the other hand, rely on insulation materials to passively maintain temperature. Both types play a key role in ensuring efficient and safe operation of EV batteries.</p></p>
