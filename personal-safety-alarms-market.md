<p><p>Personal safety alarms can be categorized into two main types: active alarms and passive alarms. Active alarms require the user to actively trigger the alarm in case of emergency, such as pressing a button or pulling a pin. Passive alarms, on the other hand, are activated automatically when certain conditions are met, such as detecting a sudden change in movement or sound. Both types aim to alert others of a potential danger and seek help for the user in emergency situations.</p></p>
