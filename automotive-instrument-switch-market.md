<p><p>Automotive instrument switches can be categorized into different types based on their functionality. Line-to-Line switches are used to connect two power lines, Line-to-Neutral switches connect one power line and one neutral line, Voltmeter switches are used to switch between different voltage readings, and Ammeter switches are used to switch between different current readings. Other types of switches may include temperature switches or pressure switches, depending on the specific requirements of the vehicle's instrumentation system.</p></p>
