<p><p>The rocker switch market is primarily divided into two types: single-pole and multipole rocker switches. Single-pole rocker switches control a single circuit, making them ideal for straightforward applications like household lighting. In contrast, multipole rocker switches can handle multiple circuits simultaneously, offering enhanced functionality for more complex systems, such as in industrial equipment or multi-light setups. Both types cater to specific needs across various sectors, including residential, commercial, and industrial applications, contributing to their market growth and versatility.</p></p>
