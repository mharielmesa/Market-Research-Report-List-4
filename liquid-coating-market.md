<p><ul><li>Solvent Borne</li><li>Water Borne</li><li>UV Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid coating market includes three main types: solvent borne, water borne, and UV cure coatings. Solvent borne coatings use organic solvents as carriers, providing excellent adhesion and durability. Water borne coatings are environmentally friendly as they use water as a carrier instead of solvents, reducing VOC emissions. UV cure coatings are cured instantly through UV radiation, offering high performance and low energy consumption. Each type caters to different needs and preferences, providing a diverse range of options for various industries and applications.</p></p>
