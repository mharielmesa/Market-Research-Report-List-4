<p><p>RFID Reader market is segmented into four types based on frequency: LF (Low Frequency) RFID Reader, HF (High Frequency) RFID Reader, UHF (Ultra High Frequency) RFID Reader, and MW (Microwave) RFID Reader. LF readers typically have short read range and are used in access control systems. HF readers have medium read range and are common in payment and ticketing applications. UHF readers have long read range and are popular in retail and supply chain sectors. MW readers have the longest read range and are utilized in specialized industrial applications.</p></p>
