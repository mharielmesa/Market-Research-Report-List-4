<p><ul><li>Single Core Cable Systems</li><li>Multicore Cable Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Submarine communication cable systems are categorized into two main types: Single Core Cable Systems and Multicore Cable Systems. Single core cable systems consist of one cable for transmitting data and information. On the other hand, Multicore cable systems involve multiple cables within the same system, allowing for the transmission of larger amounts of data simultaneously. Single core cable systems are commonly used in smaller scale communication networks, while multicore cable systems are utilized in larger scale operations requiring higher bandwidth and capacity.</p></p>
