Polyethylene Waxes are synthetic waxes produced through the polymerization of ethylene, known for their low viscosity and high melting points. They are commonly used in industries such as coatings, plastics, and adhesives. Oxidized Polyethylene Waxes are produced through the oxidation of polyethylene waxes, resulting in improved compatibility with polar materials. They are often utilized as lubricants, dispersants, and additives in various applications.</p></p>
