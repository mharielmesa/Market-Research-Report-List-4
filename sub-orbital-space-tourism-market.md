Sub-orbital reusable vehicles are spacecraft designed to reach the edge of space before returning to Earth, providing passengers with a short period of weightlessness. High-altitude balloons offer a similar experience, reaching the upper atmosphere before descending back down. Parabolic aircraft simulate zero gravity by flying in a series of steep climbs and descents. These different options cater to varying preferences and budgets within the space tourism market.</p></p>
