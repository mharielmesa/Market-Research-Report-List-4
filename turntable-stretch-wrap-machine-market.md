<p><p>Turntable stretch wrap machines are divided into three main market types: Manual stretch wrappers are operated entirely by hand, requiring the user to control the wrapping process manually. Semiautomatic stretch wrappers have some automated features, such as automatic film attachment and cutting, but still require some manual input from the user. Automatic stretch wrappers are fully automated, requiring minimal to no intervention from the user, as they control all aspects of the wrapping process independently. Each market type offers varying levels of efficiency and convenience for different packaging needs.</p></p>
