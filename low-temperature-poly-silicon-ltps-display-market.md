<p><p>Low Temperature Poly Silicon (LTPS) displays are a type of thin-film transistor (TFT) technology that utilizes low-temperature polycrystalline silicon for the backplane of the display. LTPS displays offer advantages such as faster electron mobility and enhanced pixel density, making them increasingly popular in high-end smartphones and tablets.</p><p>The Low Temperature Poly Silicon LTPS Display Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the rising demand for high-resolution displays in consumer electronics.
