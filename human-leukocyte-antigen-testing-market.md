<p><p>Human Leukocyte Antigen (HLA) testing market can be divided into two main types: molecular assay technologies and non-molecular assay technologies. Molecular assay technologies involve techniques such as PCR and sequencing to analyze specific HLA genes and alleles. Non-molecular assay technologies, on the other hand, include serological methods like complement-dependent cytotoxicity and lymphocytotoxicity assays. Both types of technologies play a crucial role in determining histocompatibility for organ transplantation and assessing autoimmune diseases.</p></p>
