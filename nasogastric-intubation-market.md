<p><ul><li>Levin Catheter</li><li>Salem Sump Catheter</li><li>Dobhoff Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nasogastric intubation market offers various types of tubes for gastric decompression and feeding. Levin catheter is a simple, single lumen tube used for gastric drainage. Salem sump catheter features an additional air vent to prevent tube collapse and mucosal damage. Dobhoff tube is a smaller, flexible tube preferred for feeding and medication delivery. Despite differences in design and usage, all three types are essential tools in managing gastrointestinal issues and ensuring proper nutrition in healthcare settings.</p></p>
