<p><ul><li>Wet Multiplate Clutches</li><li>Dry Single-Plate Clutches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Twin-clutch transmission market consists of two main types: wet multiplate clutches and dry single-plate clutches. Wet multiplate clutches use oil for cooling and lubrication, providing better heat dissipation and smoother shifting. This makes them ideal for high-performance vehicles. Dry single-plate clutches, on the other hand, do not use oil, making them lighter and more fuel efficient. They are commonly used in mainstream passenger cars. Both types offer different benefits and cater to different needs within the automotive market.</p></p>
