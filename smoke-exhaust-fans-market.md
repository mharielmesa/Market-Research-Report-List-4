<p><p>Smoke exhaust fans are categorized into three main types in the market: centrifugal type, axial type, and others. Centrifugal fans are designed to move air radially, providing high-pressure airflow suitable for applications that require ventilation over long distances. Axial fans, on the other hand, are ideal for applications that require high airflow with low pressure. The "Others" category includes specialized fans such as mixed flow fans and turbo fans that are designed for specific ventilation requirements. Each type of fan offers unique advantages depending on the application.</p></p>
