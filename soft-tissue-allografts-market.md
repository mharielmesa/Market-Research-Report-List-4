<p><p>Soft tissue allografts are types of tissue grafts used in medical procedures. These grafts can be classified into three main types based on the type of tissue they are composed of: Hyaline, Elastic, and Fibro. Hyaline grafts consist of smooth and lubricating tissue, while Elastic grafts are composed of stretchable tissue. Fibro grafts, on the other hand, are made up of strong and flexible tissue. Each type of soft tissue allograft serves specific purposes in repairing and replacing damaged or injured tissues in the body.</p></p>
