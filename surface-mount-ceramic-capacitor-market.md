<p><strong>The Surface Mount Ceramic Capacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Below 0.1μF</li><li>0.1μF～100μF</li><li>100μF～500μF</li><li>Above 500μF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface mount ceramic capacitors are classified based on capacitance value into four main categories. Capacitors below 0.1μF are used for high-frequency applications and noise filtering. Capacitors ranging from 0.1μF to 100μF are commonly used for power supply decoupling and filtering. Capacitors between 100μF and 500μF are used in power electronics for smoothing and energy storage. Capacitors above 500μF are typically used in applications requiring large amounts of energy storage, such as in power inverters and motor drives. Each type of capacitor serves a specific purpose in electronic circuits.</p></p>
