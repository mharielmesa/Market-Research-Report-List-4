<p><p>Mark pens are available in three main types - Aqueous, Oily, and Alcohol-based. Aqueous mark pens are water-based, making them ideal for use on paper as they dry quickly and do not bleed through. Oily mark pens have a thicker consistency, perfect for use on non-porous surfaces like glass or plastic. Alcohol-based mark pens provide quick drying and vibrant colors, making them suitable for a variety of surfaces. Each type of mark pen caters to different needs and preferences, offering a wide range of options for users.</p></p>
