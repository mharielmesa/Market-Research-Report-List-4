<p><ul><li>Behind the Ear (BTE)</li><li>In the Ear (ITE)</li><li>In the Canal (ITC)</li><li>Completely In Canal (CIC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bone conduction hearing implants are available in different types based on their placement on or in the ear. Behind the Ear (BTE) implants sit behind the ear, In the Ear (ITE) implants are placed within the outer ear, In the Canal (ITC) implants are placed further inside the ear canal, and Completely In Canal (CIC) implants are placed deep within the ear canal. Each type offers varying levels of comfort, discretion, and sound quality to individuals with hearing loss.</p></p>
