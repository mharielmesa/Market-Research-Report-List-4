<p><ul><li>ECG Resting System</li><li>ECG Holter Monitoring System</li><li>ECG Stress Testing System</li><li>Cardiopulmonary Stress Testing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ECG devices market includes various types of systems used for different purposes in monitoring heart activity. ECG resting systems are used for routine heart monitoring, while ECG Holter monitoring systems are portable devices for continuous monitoring. ECG stress testing systems are used to evaluate heart function during physical activity. Cardiopulmonary stress testing systems measure both heart and lung function during exercise. Each type of system plays a crucial role in diagnosing and managing heart conditions, providing valuable insights for healthcare professionals.</p></p>
