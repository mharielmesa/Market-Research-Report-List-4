<p><p>The transmitting antennas market is categorized by frequency types: HF (High Frequency), VHF (Very High Frequency), and UHF (Ultra High Frequency). HF antennas operate in the 3-30 MHz range, suitable for long-distance communication. VHF antennas, ranging from 30-300 MHz, are essential for television and radio broadcasting, providing better line-of-sight coverage. UHF antennas, operating between 300 MHz and 3 GHz, support advanced communication systems like cellular networks and satellite transmissions, catering to modern connectivity needs.</p></p>
