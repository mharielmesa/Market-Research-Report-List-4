<p><p>Remote clinical trials can be categorized into three main types: interventional, observational, and expanded access markets. Interventional trials involve testing new treatments or interventions to evaluate their efficacy and safety. Observational trials involve collecting data on a patient's health outcomes without any interference in their treatment. Expanded access markets provide experimental treatments to patients with serious or life-threatening conditions who do not qualify for traditional clinical trials. These different types cater to various research needs and patient populations in the remote clinical trial setting.</p></p>
