<p><p>The Vacuum Conveyor market consists of two main types: Integral Type and Split Type. Integral Type vacuum conveyors are designed as a single unit with all components integrated into one system, offering a compact and easy-to-use solution for material handling. On the other hand, Split Type vacuum conveyors feature separate units for the vacuum generator and transport system, allowing for more flexibility and customization in terms of installation and operation. Both types cater to different needs and applications within the material handling industry.</p></p>
