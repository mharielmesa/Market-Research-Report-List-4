<p><p>Automotive Cylinder Liners are used to protect the cylinder walls in an engine from wear and tear. There are two main types of materials used for these liners: Casting Iron and Aluminum Alloys. Casting Iron liners are known for their durability and heat resistance, making them ideal for high-performance engines. On the other hand, Aluminum Alloy liners are lightweight and offer good heat dissipation, making them suitable for reducing engine weight and increasing fuel efficiency. Both types of liners play a crucial role in ensuring the longevity and efficiency of automotive engines.</p></p>
