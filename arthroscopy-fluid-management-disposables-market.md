<p><ul><li>Pump Management Disposables</li><li>Gravity Management Disposables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Arthroscopy fluid management disposables consist of pump management disposables and gravity management disposables. Pump management disposables utilize a pump system to control the flow and pressure of irrigation fluid during arthroscopic procedures. On the other hand, gravity management disposables rely on gravity to regulate the flow of irrigation fluid. Both types of disposables play a crucial role in maintaining clear visibility and proper distension of the joint during arthroscopy procedures, ultimately aiding in the success of the surgical intervention.</p></p>
