<p><ul><li>Closed Loop AMR Current Sensor</li><li>Open Loop AMR Current Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AMR current sensor market is categorized into two main types: closed loop and open loop sensors. Closed loop AMR sensors utilize feedback mechanisms for enhanced accuracy, allowing for precise current measurements by adjusting the output based on the detected magnetic field. Conversely, open loop AMR sensors directly measure the magnetic field induced by electric current without feedback, offering a simpler and typically less expensive solution. Both types are widely used in various applications, including power monitoring and control systems.</p></p>
