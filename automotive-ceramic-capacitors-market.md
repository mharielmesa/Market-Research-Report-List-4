<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multiple-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive ceramic capacitors are categorized into two main types: single layer and multiple-layer capacitors. Single layer capacitors consist of a single ceramic layer with electrodes attached to either side, offering a simple and cost-effective solution for basic applications. On the other hand, multiple-layer capacitors are constructed with multiple ceramic layers stacked together, providing higher capacitance values and improved performance for advanced applications. Both types of capacitors play a crucial role in various automotive electronic systems.</p></p>
