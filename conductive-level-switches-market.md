<p><p>Conductive level switches are available in two main market types - integral type and split type. Integral type switches feature the electrodes and electronics housed together in one unit, making them compact and easy to install. Split type switches, on the other hand, separate the electrodes and electronics into two units for added flexibility in installation and maintenance. Both types serve the same purpose of detecting liquid levels by using conductive principles, but offer different advantages based on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
