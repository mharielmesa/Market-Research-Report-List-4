<p><p>LED inspection lights come in two main types: magnetic and non-magnetic. Magnetic inspection lights have a built-in magnet that allows them to be easily attached to metal surfaces for hands-free operation, making them convenient for lighting up work areas. On the other hand, non-magnetic inspection lights do not have this magnetic feature and may need a separate tripod or stand for support. Both types offer diverse options for professionals needing reliable, hands-free illumination during inspections and other tasks.</p></p>
