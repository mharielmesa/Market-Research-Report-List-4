<p><p>The flame retardant polyamide market is categorized into two main types: halogen type and halogen-free type. Halogen-type flame retardants include bromine or chlorine compounds, providing effective flame resistance but raising environmental and health concerns. In contrast, halogen-free type flame retardants utilize phosphorus, nitrogen, or inorganic chemicals, promoting safety and sustainability while meeting regulatory standards. Both types are crucial in applications such as automotive, electronics, and construction, where fire safety is paramount.</p></p>
