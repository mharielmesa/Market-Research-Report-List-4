<p><strong>The Agriculture Tractor Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias-ply Tires</li><li>Radial-ply Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Agriculture tractor tires market consists of two main types - Bias-ply tires and Radial-ply tires. Bias-ply tires are constructed with multiple layers of crisscrossed nylon cords, providing increased durability and stability. They are suitable for heavy-duty applications and off-road terrains. On the other hand, Radial-ply tires have a unique construction with plies running perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better traction, fuel efficiency, and overall longevity. They are preferred for high-speed operations and on-road use in agriculture tractors.</p></p>
