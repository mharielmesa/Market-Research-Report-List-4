<p><p>The intramuscular injector market features two main types: pre-fillable injection systems and fillable needle-free injectors. Pre-fillable systems simplify the injection process by allowing healthcare providers or patients to use pre-loaded syringes, enhancing convenience and safety. In contrast, fillable needle-free injectors eliminate the traditional needle, using high-pressure technology to deliver medication through the skin, reducing pain and risk of needle-stick injuries. Both types aim to improve patient compliance and streamline medication administration in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
