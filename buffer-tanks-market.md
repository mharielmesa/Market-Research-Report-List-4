<p><strong>The Buffer Tanks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chilled Water Buffer Tanks (CBT)</li><li>Hot Water Buffer Tanks (HBT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Buffer tanks are used in HVAC systems to store and regulate the flow of chilled or hot water. Chilled Water Buffer Tanks (CBT) are used in cooling systems to absorb excess capacity and maintain a stable temperature. On the other hand, Hot Water Buffer Tanks (HBT) are used in heating systems to store and supply hot water as needed. Both types of buffer tanks help to improve system efficiency, reduce energy consumption, and prevent temperature fluctuations in high-demand environments.</p></p>
