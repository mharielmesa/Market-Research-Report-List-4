<p><p>Spring Loaded Pressure Safety Valves (PSVs) are categorized into two main market types: Closed Type and Unclosed Type. Closed type PSVs are used in systems where the discharge from the valve must be contained and directed to a specific location. Unclosed type PSVs are typically used in systems where the discharge can be released into the atmosphere. Both types of PSVs serve the purpose of protecting equipment and personnel from overpressure situations by automatically releasing excess fluids or gases.</p></p>
