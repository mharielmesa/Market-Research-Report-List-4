<p><p>The car gyroscope market consists of three main types: 2 axis, 3 axis, and 6 axis gyros. A 2 axis gyroscope measures tilt or rotation around two axes, providing basic stabilization. A 3 axis gyroscope measures rotation around three axes, offering more precise control and stability. A 6 axis gyroscope combines the data from a 3 axis gyro and a 3 axis accelerometer to provide even more accurate motion sensing capabilities. Each type of gyroscope caters to different needs and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
