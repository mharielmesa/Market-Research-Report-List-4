<p><p>The small UAV market can be categorized into three main types: fixed-wing, helicopter, and rotary blade. Fixed-wing UAVs are similar to airplanes, with a longer flight endurance and larger payload capacity. Helicopter UAVs are capable of vertical take-offs and landings, making them ideal for hovering and maneuvering in tight spaces. Rotary blade UAVs have multiple rotors that provide stability and agility, allowing them to fly in various directions. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different applications within the small UAV market.</p></p>
