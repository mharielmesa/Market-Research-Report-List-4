<p><p>Signal converters are electronic devices that facilitate the conversion of signals in various forms such as analog to digital or digital to analog. An Analog-Digital Converter (ADC) market focuses on converting continuous analog signals into discrete digital signals for processing and analysis. On the other hand, a Digital Analog Converter (DAC) market deals with converting digital signals back into analog form for output. Both types of converters play a crucial role in various industries such as telecommunications, consumer electronics, and industrial automation.</p></p>
