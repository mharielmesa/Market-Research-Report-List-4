<p><p>Portable Medical Vacuum System market includes three main types of technologies: Dry Claw Pump Technology, Dry Rotary Vane Technology, and Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology. </p><p>Dry Claw Pump Technology uses two claw-shaped rotors to compress air without the use of oil, making it suitable for medical applications where contamination is a concern. </p><p>Dry Rotary Vane Technology utilizes rotary vanes to create vacuum pressure without oil, making it compact and low maintenance.</p><p>Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology uses oil for lubrication and sealing, offering high performance and durability in medical vacuum systems.</p></p>
