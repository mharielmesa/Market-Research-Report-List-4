<p><p>Mixed current spray drying equipment can be classified into various types based on their atomization method and design. Some common types include rotary atomizer, nozzle atomizer, fluidized, closed loop, centrifugal, and others. Rotary atomizers use centrifugal force to generate droplets, while nozzle atomizers create a fine mist through pressure. Fluidized spray dryers use a high-velocity gas stream to suspend and dry the product. Closed-loop systems recycle drying gas, while centrifugal spray dryers use centrifugal force for atomization. Other types may include hybrid designs or specialized technologies.</p></p>
