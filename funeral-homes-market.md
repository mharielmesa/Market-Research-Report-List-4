<p><p>Funeral homes operate in two primary market types: At-Need and Pre-Need. At-Need refers to services provided at the time of death, where families require immediate assistance and support for funeral arrangements. Pre-Need, on the other hand, involves pre-planning and pre-paying for funeral services before death occurs. This allows individuals to make decisions in advance, easing the burden on loved ones and ensuring their wishes are carried out. Both market types play a crucial role in the funeral industry, catering to different needs and preferences of clients.</p></p>
