<p><p>Parcel conveyor systems are widely used in industries for efficient material handling. The two main types of parcel conveyor systems are roller conveyors and belt conveyors. Roller conveyors use multiple rollers to transport parcels, while belt conveyors use a continuous belt to move items. Roller conveyors are best suited for handling heavier items, while belt conveyors are ideal for moving parcels quickly and smoothly. Both types of conveyor systems play a crucial role in streamlining operations and optimizing workflow in various industries.</p></p>
