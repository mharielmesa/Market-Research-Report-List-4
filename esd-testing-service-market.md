<p><p>ESD Testing Service Market includes various types such as Resistance & Resistivity, Static Decay, Charge Generation, Static Shielding, and others. Resistance & Resistivity testing measures the electrical resistance of a material. Static Decay testing evaluates how quickly a material discharges static electricity. Charge Generation testing assesses how much static charge a material can generate. Static Shielding testing measures a material's ability to block static electricity. Other types may include surface resistance testing, voltage threshold testing, and capacitance testing.</p></p>
