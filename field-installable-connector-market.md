<p><p>Field Installable Connector Market Types refer to two main categories: Mechanical Type and Fusion Splice Type. The Mechanical Type connectors are typically easier to install as they do not require any splicing or fusion. They simply align and connect fiber optic cables using a mechanical method. Fusion Splice Type connectors, on the other hand, involve fusing or splicing the fiber optic cables together for a more permanent and secure connection. Both types cater to different installation requirements and preferences in the market.</p></p>
