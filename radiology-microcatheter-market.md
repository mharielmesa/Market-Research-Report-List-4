<p><p>Radiology microcatheter market can be divided into four main types. Diagnostic microcatheters are used for imaging and diagnosing blockages or abnormalities in blood vessels. Delivery microcatheters are designed to deliver medication or medical devices to specific areas within the body. Steerable microcatheters can navigate through narrow or intricate blood vessels with precision. Aspiration microcatheters are used to remove blood clots or other obstructions from blood vessels. Each type serves a unique purpose in interventional radiology procedures.</p></p>
