<p><ul><li>Standard Tablet Coating Pan</li><li>Perforated Tablet Coating Pan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of coating pans used in the pharmaceutical industry: standard tablet coating pans and perforated tablet coating pans. Standard tablet coating pans are solid, smooth drums used for coating tablets with a uniform layer of film or sugar. Perforated tablet coating pans, on the other hand, have small holes or perforations on the drum surface to allow for better ventilation and drying of tablets during the coating process. Both types of coating pans are essential for achieving high-quality coatings on pharmaceutical tablets.</p></p>
