<p><p>The Motorcycle Brake By Wire (BBW) Systems market is segmented into single-channel and multiple-channel systems. Single-channel systems use one control unit to operate both front and rear brakes, simplifying the design and reducing costs. On the other hand, multiple-channel systems have separate control units for the front and rear brakes, providing more precise control and improved safety. The choice between single-channel and multiple-channel systems depends on factors such as cost, complexity, and desired level of performance for the motorcycle brake system.</p></p>
