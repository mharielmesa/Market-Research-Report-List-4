<p><p>Automatic Vehicle Fire Suppression Systems are crucial for protecting vehicles from fire hazards. The Wet System involves a network of pipes filled with water or fire retardant, which is activated automatically in case of a fire. The Dry System uses pressurized gas or powder to suppress the fire. Both systems are effective in extinguishing flames quickly and preventing extensive damage to vehicles. The choice between Wet and Dry Systems depends on the type of vehicle and the specific fire risks it may face.</p></p>
