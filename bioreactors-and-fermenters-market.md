<p><p>The bioreactors and fermenters market is categorized into single-use and multiple-use bioreactors. Single-use bioreactors are designed for one-time use, minimizing cross-contamination and reducing cleaning requirements, making them ideal for small-scale production and fast development cycles. In contrast, multiple-use bioreactors are built for repeated use, offering durability and versatility for larger-scale operations. They require stringent cleaning processes but are cost-effective over time. Both types play crucial roles in biopharmaceutical production, food processing, and other biotechnological applications.</p></p>
