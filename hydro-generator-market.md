<p><p>Hydro generators can be classified into two main types based on the type of turbine used: Impulse Turbine and Reaction Turbine. Impulse Turbines rely on the force of flowing water to generate power, converting kinetic energy into mechanical energy. In contrast, Reaction Turbines use both kinetic and potential energy of the water to produce electricity. Both types play a crucial role in the hydro generator market, offering different advantages depending on factors such as water flow and head.</p></p>
