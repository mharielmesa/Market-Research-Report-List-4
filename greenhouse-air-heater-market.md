<p><p>Greenhouse air heaters can be categorized into two types based on the ignition mechanism: electronic ignition and manual ignition. Electronic ignition heaters use a built-in electronic system to ignite the heater, providing a more convenient and automated operation. On the other hand, manual ignition heaters require users to manually ignite the heater using a handheld ignition tool. Both types offer effective heating solutions for greenhouses, but electronic ignition heaters are typically preferred for their ease of use and efficient performance.</p></p>
