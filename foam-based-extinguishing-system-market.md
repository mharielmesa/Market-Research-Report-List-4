<p><p>Foam-based extinguishing systems are categorized into three types based on the expansion level of the foam used: Low Expansion, Medium Expansion, and High Expansion. Low expansion systems are suitable for enclosed spaces with minimal air movement, while Medium expansion systems are more effective in open areas with moderate ventilation. High expansion systems are ideal for large, open spaces where rapid fire suppression is required. Each type offers unique benefits depending on the specific fire risk and environment.</p></p>
